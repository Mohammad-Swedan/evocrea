A 12-branch retail chain came to us with a critical operational problem: their customer management was completely fragmented. Sales reps across branches were using different WhatsApp groups, personal spreadsheets, and sticky notes to track leads. Every week, deals were slipping through the cracks.

We ran a 5-day discovery sprint, mapped every touchpoint in their customer journey, and designed a system architecture that would become the operational backbone of their entire sales operation.
